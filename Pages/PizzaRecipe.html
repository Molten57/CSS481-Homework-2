My mom and I have a homemade pizza recipe
here are the ingredients you need and
the steps on how to make it yourself!

Ingredients

Table: Ingredients for a pizza
Ingredient Amount
Medium sized uncut Peta Bread 1
Ketchup at least 4oz
Mayonnaise at least 4oz
Pepperoni up to preference
Olives up to preference
Cut-up Bell Peppers up to preference

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300-350 degrees
  2. Mix-up Ketchup & Mayonnaise in a bowl
  3. Spread the mix over Peta Bread
  4. Add ingredients ontop like Pepperoni, Olives, etc.
  5. Heat Pizza in oven for 10-12 minutes
  6. Cut up into slices and enjoy!
